Be a Sponsor

Partner with Victory Cricket Club and support a thriving, inclusive cricket community in Derby. Your sponsorship helps us develop players, improve facilities, and deliver memorable matchday experiences.

Start Sponsoring

Why Sponsor VCC

  • Reach local families, players, and supporters across Derbyshire.
  • Brand visibility on club kits, events, and digital channels.
  • Support youth development and women’s cricket.
  • Build community goodwill and long-term partnerships.

What We Offer

  • Logo placement on matchday banners, kits, and training wear.
  • Social media shout-outs and website recognition.
  • On-ground branding at home fixtures and events.
  • Custom packages aligned to your business goals.

Sponsorship Packages

Contact us for pricing and availability.

Title Sponsor (Minimum 2 Years)

  • Logo displayed on the front of our club cricket jerseys
  • Promotion on the club website
  • Social media promotion of your adverts or posters
  • Networking opportunities at matches and club events
  • Advertising at the ground and on event days

Gold Sponsor (Minimum 2 Years)

  • Logo on the back or sleeve of the jersey
  • Banner display on match days
  • Sponsor listing on the club website
  • Social media mentions

Silver Sponsor

  • Banner display on match days
  • Sponsor listing on the club website
  • Social media mentions
  • Option to add sponsor logo on: Kit bags (11) or Cricket bats (on the side) (11) or Stumps

Bronze Sponsor

  • Sponsor listing on the club website
  • Sharing your promotions and offers on our social media platforms and club WhatsApp group

Other Special Categories

  • Catering Partner: All catering orders for club events at an agreed discounted rate
  • Sponsor listing on the club website
  • Sharing your promotions and offers on our social media platforms and club WhatsApp group
  • Women’s Team Sponsor (Minimum 2 Years): Logo on the back or sleeve of the women’s team jersey
  • Sponsor Trophies: Brand or firm name engraved on all Man of the Match trophies
  • Social media mentions
